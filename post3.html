Football:My favourite team and player

Posted by Devesh Paragiri on August 24, 2018

How it all began

I have always enjoyed playing football since I was a young child.I like football because it’s simple; anyone can understand it, follow it. The rules are easy to understand so it’s very democratic.I started off playing in the streets as a free time activity.When I came to knew about a school football team I was excited.The summer coaching started off during the hot month of May.Yet the heat did not deter me and a group of young boys playing to our hearts content.There began my journey with football.I began attending interschool football tournaments when I was 9 years of age.

My first win

I was 12 years of age when I won my first football tournament.I was very happy and thanked my football coach for all the support.I went on to win the player of the tournament for my perfomance.

My favourite team and player

It all started when I watched the match between Real Madrid and Barcelona.From then on I liked watching Real Madrid play and my favourite player and team became Cristiano Ronaldo and his team,Real Madrid.
